Fried Chicken Sandwich

Description

This fried chicken sandwich is ultra crispy on the outside, tender & juicy on the inside and bursting with flavour all over!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thigh
  • Burger Bun
  • Mayo
  • Lettuce
  • Pickles

Steps

  1. Dip the chicken thigh in batter and fry it for 10-12 minutes
  2. Prepare the sauce by adding mustard, hot sauce and honey to mayo
  3. Toast the buns and apply sauce
  4. Add the chicken, pickles and lettuce
  5. Serve!
